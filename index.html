ABOUT

Established in Brunei Darussalam in 1965, Sin Guan Brothers Co. (SGBC) has proudly become one of the major wholesalers in agricultural products, food and beverages, confectionery and cleaning products all across Brunei. It has now become a leader in the wholesale market.

From humble origins, of a small shop in Jalan Sultan, the founder singlehandedly built this business success story/venture. We appreciate and understand the value of the hard work and perseverance.

SGBC now importssss products from ASEAN as well as other countries internationally such as China, New Zealand, Denmark, Holland, Canada, Turkey and a few other European countries. We aim to serve and provide our customers with the best products and premier services. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.
