About

Established in Brunei Darussalam in 1965, Sin Guan Brothers Co. (SGBC) has proudly become one of the major wholesalers in agricultural products, food and beverages, confectionery and cleaning products all across Brunei. It has now become a leader in the wholesale market.

With humble origins, the founder single-handedly managed a small shop once located in Jalan Sultan. We appreciate and understand the value of the hard work and perseverance.

SGBC now import products from ASEAN as well as other countries internationally such as China, New Zealand, Denmark, Holland, Canada, Turkey and a few other European countries. We aim to serve and provide our customers with the best products and premier services. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.
